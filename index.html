Featured Bands

Magoria:

Magoria is a heavy metal band originating from Spain, formed in 2011. Drawing influences ranging from classic heavy metal to progressive metal.


For All We Know:

The music of For All We Know is a fusion of various styles, spanning from progressive metal and alternative rock to symphonic metal.

A New Dawn:

A New Dawn is a symphonic metal band originating from Spain, characterized by epic melodies and lyrics that explore themes of fantasy and deep emotions.

Alter Bridge:

Alter Bridge is a rock band hailing from the United States, renowned for its powerful sound and emotive lyrics. Comprised of former members of Creed and talented musicians.

Arch Enemy:

Arch Enemy is a Swedish melodic death metal band that was formed in 1995. They are renowned for their aggressive yet melodic style, as well as their technical approach and instrumental virtuosity.


Ayreon:

Ayreon is a Dutch musical project created by musician and composer Arjen Anthony Lucassen. Established in 1995, Ayreon is renowned for its focus on concepts and conceptual albums that blend a wide range of musical styles.

Anubis Gate:

Anubis Gate is a Danish progressive metal band formed in 2001 in Copenhagen, Denmark. The band is characterized by its unique approach to progressive metal, blending elements from various subgenres such as power metal, symphonic metal, and melodic metal.

Black Rose Maze:

Black Rose Maze is a musical project led by the talented Canadian vocalist Rosa Laricchiuta. The band blends elements of hard rock and melodic rock to create a distinctive and powerful sound.
